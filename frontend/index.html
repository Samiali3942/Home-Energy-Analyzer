About This Project

This Energy Consumption Dashboard is designed to help households understand and optimize their energy usage. By analyzing consumption patterns, identifying energy-intensive appliances, and providing actionable recommendations, we aim to promote energy efficiency and sustainability.

The dashboard uses real-time data from smart meters and IoT devices to track energy consumption across different appliances. This data is then processed and visualized to provide meaningful insights that can help you make informed decisions about your energy usage.

Our goal is to empower households with the knowledge and tools they need to reduce their carbon footprint and contribute to a more sustainable future. By making small changes to your energy consumption habits, you can make a big difference to both your wallet and the environment.
